Best Brass Instruments

Brass instruments are made of brass (of course) and is played by buzzing your lips into a mouthpiece attached to one end of the instrument. By changing the shape of your lips and using different valves or a slide, you can control the pitch and produce different notes.

Euphonium

The euphonium has great tone colour, and has a very warm sound. Therefore it is on this list.

Cornet

The cornet has beutiful tone colour, and is a very high pitched instrument. It is on this list because it is the best brass instrument to play notes in the high register.

Trombone

The trombone's sound isn't as round as other brass instruments, but it is so unique in the fact that it uses a slide to play different notes as opposed to valves. It is on this list because it is so unique and its uniquness can be used.

Tuba

The tuba has the warmest tone colour as it is the biggest and grandest instrument, and has a very warm sound. Therefore it is on this list.
