Best String Instruments

String instruments create sound by making strings vibrate. One plays a string instrument by plucking, strumming, or bowing the strings with your fingers or a bowing it with a bow commonly made of horsehair.

Cello

The Cello is the one of best string instrument because of it's tone colour, and the fact that it is included in many ensembles unlike the harp.

Harp

The harp is a very nice sounding instrument and it has lots of strings and pedals.

Viola

The viola is a bowed string instrument that is mainly played in alto clef which makes it unique. It has a very nice tone colour at its pitch is between that of a violin and a cello. Because of this, it sounds very similar to them, but it still is, nonetheless, pretty.

Violin

The violin is a bowed and plucked string instrument that is mainly play in treble clef. It is included in many ensembled such as chamber music and orchestras. It has a very nice tembre when played well with beautiful vibrato.
