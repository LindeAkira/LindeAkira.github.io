Best Percussion Instruments

A percussion musical instrument is a super diverse and exciting category of instruments that you play by hitting, shaking, or scraping them to create awesome rhythms and sounds. They're like the heartbeat of music, providing a strong and steady pulse that keeps everything grooving.

Piano

The is a very popular instrument because it can be played by itself. Because you can play multiple notes at once, there is a lot of repertoire, with many beutiful pieces that don't require accompaniment.

Marimba

Similarly to the piano, the marimba can also play multiple notes at once but not as many as the piano. It has a very interesting and pretty tone colour which can be changed by the types of mallets used.

Vibraphone

The vibraphone has great tone colour, and has the roundest and most soothing sound out of all percussion instruments

Drum Kit

The drum kit is one of the most important percussion instruments because it serves as the backbone most contemporary mucisc. It can also be used and many different genres of contemporary music such as jazz, rock, metal etc.
