Best Woodwind Instruments

Woodwind instruments are instruments that produces sound when you blow air through it. Despite the name, not all woodwind instruments are made entirely of wood; some have metal parts too. What makes them unique is the fact that they all use wooden reeds that vibrat (some even use double reeds!).

Oboe

The Oboe has great tone colour, and has a very warm sound. Therefore it is on this list.

Soprano Saxophone

The Soprano Saxophone is one of the best jazz instruments because of it's unique tone colour, and robust range.

Bassoon

The Bassoon is a very interesting instrument as it is double reeded. This gives it an interesting tone colour. It is one of the best instruments because of its unique tembre.

Clarinet

The Clarinet is a single reeded instrument which, when played well, has a very beautiful tone colour. It is on this list because it has a very big range, is included in many bands, and overall, osunds very nice.

Flute

The Flute is a unique instrument because you have to blow into it at the perfect andgle to make a sound. It mostly plays high notes and it requires a lot of practice to be played well, but sounds very nice.
